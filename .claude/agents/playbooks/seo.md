## What's working now (benchmarks) — last updated 2026-07-13
- Finance/YMYL AI Overview trigger rate is low (~16% of finance queries), and
  when AI Overviews do fire, citations skew to highly authoritative/expert
  sources — our live-data + explicit-verdict format is a good fit but only if
  the page has real structure (headings, stated numbers, dated data) for the
  crawler to lift.
- Since the Dec-2025 core update, E-E-A-T baseline applies beyond strict YMYL
  to all content categories — "experience" signals (named source, live pulled
  numbers, dated snapshot) now matter for a hardware-payback post, not just
  for advice pages.
- Semantic completeness and multimodal (text+chart+table) pages get cited
  meaningfully more; a bare BLUF paragraph with no headings/table is not
  snippet- or AI-Overview-ready even if the verdict is correct.
- 47% of AI Overview citations now come from pages ranking below position #5 —
  we don't need to out-rank incumbents outright to get cited, but we do need
  the on-page structure (clear threshold table, dated numbers) present.
